About Us

Unbeatable taste! That’s what gave birth to Modi Snacks in 2006 in Muzzafarpur, Bihar by Mr. Modi. Today, Modi Snacks is a brand that has emerged as the right choice of snacks & namkeen with a wide distribution network across India that is promoted by Vikash Foods Products, an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company under the leadership of his Director’s Mr. Vikash Modi & Mr. Vikram Modi.


In 2010, the company got a revamp as it was equipped with the latest hi-tech packaging machinery and an infrastructure of over 10,000 sq.ft. With an automatic line of Fryums, state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated product research & production team, we ensure that each and every pack of Modi Snacks is passed through the most stringent of quality tests before hitting the market.
